Results Section

 
Users can select the Results tab or select a sub-page under the Results header within the side navigation to view the Results section. Based on the selected standard or model, the Results section may display a variety of charts, tables, checklists, and reports summarizing the responses provided in the Assessment section.
 

Results Menu

  • The following sub-menu pages may display based on the selected assessment:
  • Results pages (summaries, checklists, charts, etc.)
  • High-Level Assessment Description, Executive Summary, and Comments
  • Reports
  • Feedback
